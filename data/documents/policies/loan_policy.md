# Personal Loan Policy

## Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for a personal loan, applicants must meet the following requirements:

- Age: 21 to 60 years
- Employment: Salaried or self-employed with minimum 2 years experience
- Minimum monthly income: ₹25,000
- Credit score: Minimum 700
- Existing loan obligations should not exceed 50% of income

## Required Documents

Please submit the following documents:

1. Identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN Card, Passport)
2. Address proof (Utility bills, Rent agreement)
3. Income proof (Salary slips, IT returns, bank statements)
4. Employment proof (Appointment letter, business documents)

## Loan Amount and Tenure

- Minimum loan amount: ₹50,000
- Maximum loan amount: ₹25,00,000
- Loan tenure: 12 to 60 months

## Application Process

1. Visit our website or mobile app
2. Fill the online application form
3. Upload required documents
4. Our team will verify your application
5. Approval typically takes 24-48 hours
6. Loan amount will be disbursed to your account

For more information, contact our customer care or visit your nearest branch.
